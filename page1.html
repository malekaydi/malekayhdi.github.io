- The story of the game:

Trapped in the university prayer room at dawn, you have a limited time to get out! As the first light
comes in, the calm quickly turns tense. Search the roomand its corners for the elusive key to unlock
the door.
Use your problem-solving skills to tackle challenges and find hidden items that will help you exit the university.
Time is running out, can you find the key and make your way out?

- Features of Dawn of escape:

Unique Setting: A university prayer room at dawn, combining calm and suspense.
Time Pressure: Players must escape before time runs out, adding excitement.
Collaborative Puzzles: Teamwork is essential to solving hidden clues and unlocking the door.
Immersive Atmosphere: The design and lighting create a tense, engaging environment.
